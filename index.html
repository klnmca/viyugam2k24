Debugging Using Java

Instruction

  • Each team must consist of only 1 member. No team-based participation is allowed.
  • The total time allocated for this round will be 1 hour. Participants must complete their tasks within this time limit.
  • You will be provided with a set of programs that contain both syntactical and logical errors. Your task is not to rewrite the entire code from scratch but to modify and correct the logic as per the given requirements. Focus on fixing the existing code rather than reconstructing it entirely.
  • You are not allowed to display the output using simple print statements (e.g., System.out.println). The output must be displayed according to the specified instructions provided during the event.
  • A total of 10 programs will be provided. Each program will contain errors, and your goal will be to identify and fix those errors.
  • The participant who completes all 10 programs within the allotted time and finishes first among the other participants will be declared the winner. Other participants will be ranked based on the number of programs completed and the time taken to complete them.
  • Internet access will not be provided, and the use of mobile phones or any other electronic devices is strictly prohibited during the event. Ensure that you are prepared to solve the problems without external assistance.
  • Any participant found using unfair means, such as cheating or attempting to bypass the rules, will be immediately disqualified from the event. Please maintain the integrity of the competition.

Venue : MCA Lab 2
